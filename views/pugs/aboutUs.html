Team IntelliJent

We are Information Technology students of Saint Louis University. This system is called IntelliDocs which is in partial fulfillment for the course IT Project.

The rationale behind implementing the IntelliDocs at SLU is to address the challenges associated with traditional paper-based document management systems, such as redundant form-making, inconsistent format, late submissions, lack of storage, document duplication, security, and no centralized repository
